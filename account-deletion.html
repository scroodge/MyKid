Request Account Deletion

MyKid · Last updated: February 2025

You can delete your MyKid account and associated data in two ways:

1. In-App (recommended)

Open the app → Settings → Account → Delete account. This removes your user and data from the database immediately.

2. By Email

Send an email to request account deletion. Include the email address used for your account.

Request account deletion via email

We will process your request within 30 days and confirm when your data has been removed.
