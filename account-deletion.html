Request Account Deletion

MyKid — baby journal app · Last updated: February 2025

Use this page to request deletion of your MyKid account and associated data.

How to request account deletion

Option 1: In the app (recommended)

  1. Open the MyKid app on your device.
  2. Go to Settings → Account.
  3. Tap Delete account and confirm.
  4. Your account and data are removed immediately.

Option 2: By email

  1. Send an email to scroodgemac@gmail.com with subject "Account Deletion Request".
  2. Include the email address used for your MyKid account.
  3. We will process your request within 30 days and confirm by email.

Data that will be deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed from our servers:

  • Your account and authentication credentials
  • Children profiles (names, birth dates, avatars)
  • Journal entries (text, dates, linked photos metadata)
  • Your membership in any family/household
  • Stored Immich server URL and API key (if saved to family)

Data that is not deleted

The following data is not stored or controlled by MyKid and will not be deleted:

  • Photos and videos — Stored in your own Immich instance. Delete them manually in Immich if desired.
  • Local data on your device — Caches and settings. Remove the app or use "Start from scratch" to clear.

Retention

Deleted data is removed permanently. We do not retain backups of deleted accounts. Processing of email requests: within 30 days.
