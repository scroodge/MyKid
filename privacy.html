Privacy Policy

MyKid · Last updated: February 2025

MyKid is an open-source baby journal app. Your privacy is important to us.

Data Storage

MyKid is designed for self-hosted use:

  • Photos and videos are stored in your Immich instance. We do not store or access your media.
  • Journal entries and children profiles are stored in your Supabase project. We do not host or access your data.

Data We Do Not Collect

When you use your own Supabase and Immich:

  • We do not collect personal data, analytics, or tracking.
  • We do not sell or share any user data.

Third-Party Services

You configure your own services:

  • Supabase — authentication, database. See Supabase Privacy.
  • Immich — photo storage. Your instance, your data. See Immich.

Your Rights (GDPR)

You can:

  • Export your data — use the in-app "Export my data" option.
  • Delete your account — use the in-app "Delete account" option. This removes your user and associated data from your Supabase project.

Contact

For privacy questions: support@mykidapp.com
